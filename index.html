About

I am Mark Mutugi — a videographer and visual storyteller driven by motion, narrative, and cinematic language.

My work lives primarily in videography and documentary form, shaping stories through composition, rhythm, and emotional pacing.

Alongside this, I explore 3D animation and modeling using Maya — building environments, lighting, and lightweight motion to extend storytelling beyond the lens.

My vision is to create work that immerses, connects, and evolves with the changing dynamics of contemporary creativity.
