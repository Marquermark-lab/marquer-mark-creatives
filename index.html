About

I am Mark Mutugi — a videographer and visual storyteller focused on motion, atmosphere, and narrative-driven work. My practice leans heavily into videography, documentary language, and cinematic composition.

I also work with 3D — Maya-based modeling, lighting, and lightweight animation — to extend storytelling beyond the physical lens.

My vision is to bring stories and ideas to life in ways that immerse, connect, and evolve with the ever-changing creative landscape.
